externality

Hi, I've built this site for my own use but decided to make things available to anyone that might find it useful. This is where I keep track of links, articles, books, try out code, or just play around with and test stuff. Named it externality because I think it's an important concept to take into account when doing anything. Could have gone with my own name but decided to just redirect that URL to this site.

The site is simple, no moving parts. Code can be found in the respository. You also have a bunch of links to some projects, my Twitter/LinkedIn/GitHub, and the WP instance used as a library. Feel free to send me an email.

For those curious about the definition of externality, here it is:

externality: a consequence of an industrial or commercial activity which affects other parties without this being reflected in market prices, such as the pollination of surrounding crops by bees kept for honey
